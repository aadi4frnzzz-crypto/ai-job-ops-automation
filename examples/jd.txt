Position: Data Analyst
Company: Flipkart
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka (Hybrid)

About the Role:
Flipkart is hiring a Data Analyst to join our Supply Chain Analytics team. You will work closely with operations, product, and business teams to drive data-driven decisions at scale.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports for supply chain KPIs (order fill rate, OTIF, warehouse utilization)
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and analyse data from large datasets (BigQuery, Redshift)
- Develop Python scripts for data automation, reporting pipelines, and ad-hoc analysis
- Collaborate with product managers and operations leads to define metrics and translate business questions into analytical frameworks
- Present findings and recommendations to senior stakeholders (VPs, Directors)
- Own the end-to-end analytics lifecycle: from requirement gathering to delivery

Requirements:
- 2-5 years of experience in data analysis, business analysis, or a related role
- Strong SQL skills (window functions, CTEs, multi-table joins)
- Proficiency in Python (pandas, numpy, data manipulation)
- Experience with BI tools (Tableau, Looker, or Superset)
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
- Experience working in e-commerce, logistics, or supply chain is a strong plus
- Familiarity with cloud data warehouses (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with dbt or data modeling tools
- Knowledge of A/B testing methodologies
- Exposure to machine learning concepts

Compensation: 12-18 LPA based on experience
